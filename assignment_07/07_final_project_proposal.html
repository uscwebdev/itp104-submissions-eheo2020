Final Project Proposal: Korean Culture and UNESCO Heritage

Project Brief

The website is dedicated to unraveling the cultural richness of South Korea's UNESCO Heritage sites, shedding light on their historical significance and cultural value. Its purpose is to serve as a guide for those unfamiliar with these remarkable locations and Korean heritage, aiming to spark interest and encourage people to visit these sites in person.

The target audience for the website encompasses individuals from diverse backgrounds who may not be well-versed in South Korean culture or UNESCO Heritage sites. It is designed for curious travelers, cultural enthusiasts, and anyone eager to explore and understand the historical and cultural significance of South Korea's unique landmarks. The content is tailored to be informative and engaging, making it accessible and appealing to a broad audience interested in discovering the rich tapestry of South Korea's heritage.

Design

  • Minimal and simple website structure and design for easy reading
