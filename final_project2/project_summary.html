Final Project Summary

Where Tradition Meets Timeless Beauty:
Explore the Rich Tapestry of South Korea's UNESCO Heritage

Topic and Purpose

The website showcases South Korea's UNESCO Heritage sites and their cultural importance. By introducing the ultural richness of South Korea's UNESCO Heritage sites, it aims to serve as an accessible guide for audiences unfamiliar with these remarkable locations and the broader spectrum of Korean culture. The goal is to inspire and guide visitors to explore these unique sites in person, fostering a deeper connection with South Korea's rich heritage.

The target audience for the website encompasses individuals from diverse backgrounds who may not be well-versed in South Korean culture or UNESCO Heritage sites. It is designed for curious travelers, cultural enthusiasts, and anyone eager to explore and understand the historical and cultural significance of South Korea's unique landmarks. The content is tailored to be informative and engaging, making it accessible and appealing to a broad audience interested in discovering the rich tapestry of South Korea's heritage.
